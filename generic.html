About Gift Akande

Gift is a data analyst with a diverse and dynamic set of skills that make me the professional you've been searching for. With a strong foundation in data management systems such as SQL, PowerBi, Tableau and Excel, I have the technical prowess needed to tackle complex data challenges. I am well-versed in data integration, transformation, and ETL processes, making me a go-to expert for these critical tasks. With half a decade of experience, I have had the privilege of working with top-tier organizations, including Knurów Hospital in Poland. There, I played a pivotal role in developing and implementing the Electronic Health Record (EHR) system, which improved data accessibility and reduced query times by 30%. My experience also includes analyzing healthcare data within the EHR alongside cross-functional teams to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of patient care.

What truly sets me apart is my track record of achievements. I developed specialized learning methods and materials that significantly aided their learning experience. I have consistently delivered results like these throughout my career, whether it’s optimizing data pipelines for efficiency or creating data-driven strategies that yield measurable outcomes. Beyond my skills and accomplishments, my personality traits make me an invaluable addition to any team. I am known for my unwavering attention to detail, which ensures data accuracy and reliability. I thrive in fast-paced environments, where my problem-solving skills shine as I tackle complex data challenges head-on. Additionally, my strong communication skills enable me to bridge the gap between technical and non-technical stakeholders, fostering collaboration and ensuring that data insights drive informed decision-making.
